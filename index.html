About Us

The circus was founded in 1960 by a troupe of London artists.Among them, acrobats, contortionists, jugglers, tamer, clowns, fakirs, magicians and illusionists. Our popular live shows feature dressage and taming numbers as well as clown shows and magic tricks. On the program, trampolines, trapeze, bicycle, fire-eater, sword-swallower, knife-thrower and mime. All our performances are accompanied by music thanks to a traditional orchestra.
